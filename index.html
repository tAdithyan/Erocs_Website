Our Training Programs

01

Level 1: Basics of Robotics

+
  • What is a Robot? (Definition, types, real-world applications)
  • Basic Components of a Robot (Motors, sensors, batteries, circuits, actuators)
  • Introduction to Microcontrollers (Arduino basics, Embedded C)
  • Basic Circuit Designing (Using Breadboards, Resistors, LEDs, Switches)
  • Simple Hands-on Projects
02

Level 2: Intermediate Robotics (Advance from Level 1)

+
  • Working with More Components (Ultrasonic sensors, IR sensors, Bluetooth module)
  • Embedded C Programming (Deeper Concepts)
  • Basic PCB Designing (Using EasyEDA/Fritzing)
  • Building Intermediate Robots
03

Level 3: Advanced Robotics (Microprocessors, AI & Simulation)

+
  • Microprocessors vs. Microcontrollers (Introduction to Raspberry Pi)
  • Introduction to AI & ML in Robotics (How AI improves robot decision-making)
  • ROS (Robot Operating System) Basics
  • Computer Vision with OpenCV (Object detection, face recognition)
  • CAD Designing for Robotics (Fusion 360/SolidWorks for 3D Robot Modeling)
  • Proteus for Circuit Simulation (Virtual testing of circuits)
  • AI-Based Robotics Projects
04

Level 4: Advanced Technologies & Human-Computer Interaction

+
  • ROS Advanced Topics (Path Planning, SLAM, Sensor Fusion)
  • AI-Powered Humanoid & Assistive Robotics
  • Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCI) & Gesture Control Robotics
  • Industry 4.0 & Robotics (AI in Automation & Smart Factories)
  • IoT & Edge Computing for Robotics
  • Final Capstone Project

Frequently Asked Questions

What is robotics?

Robotics is the science of designing, building, and programming robots for various applications..

Who can join EROCS Training Program?

Anyone! We offer beginner to advanced training section for students, professionals, and hobbyists.

Do I need programming knowledge?

No prior experience is required for beginner Training. Advanced Training may need Python or C++.

Will I get hands-on experience?

Yes! We provide practical training with Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and AI robotics kits.

Do you provide certifications?

Yes! Students receive an industry-recognized certificate upon Training completion.

What careers can robotics lead to?

Jobs in AI, automation, drone technology, industrial robotics, and software development.

Can I work on my own robotics project?

Yes! We support students in developing personal or academic projects.

Do you offer internships or job placements?

Yes! We Provide opportunities for students to work with us and gain hands-on experience in our company.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes! We provide both live online sessions and in-person training.
